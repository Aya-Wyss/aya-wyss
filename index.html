About Us

Aya Wyss was founded with a simple mission: to make premium fashion production easier and more sustainable for brands worldwide.

Based between London and Istanbul, we specialize in jerseywear, ready-to-wear collections, and eco-conscious textiles, delivering scalable manufacturing solutions for ambitious brands.

Our Services

Fabric & Garment Design

Trend-driven designs based on in-depth research, fashion weeks, and 3D technology for faster development.

Sourcing & Manufacturing

Reliable, sustainable production with trusted suppliers, ERP-managed operations, and strict quality control.

Merchandising Support

Strategic guidance from product development to retail to maximize your brand’s market success.

Sustainability Consulting

Eco-conscious solutions using organic, recycled, and low-impact materials to meet modern sustainability goals.

Why Choose Us
